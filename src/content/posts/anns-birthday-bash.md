# Ann’s Birthday Bash (tasting notes)

This was a simple birthday get-together: a handful of bottles, plenty of conversation, and the kind of relaxed pacing that lets you return to a dram and see something new.

## How the line-up drank

Rather than focusing on one distillery, we went for a **mixed flight**—a couple of easy sippers to start, then richer, sweeter styles later in the evening.

If I had to summarise the theme: **friendly whiskies with comfort flavours** (vanilla, fruit, soft spice) and just enough variety to keep your palate awake.

---

## What stood out

- A **mixed flight** with variety kept everyone engaged.
- The **sherried pour** was the best match with birthday cake.
- The **surprise guest bottle** sparked the most discussion.

---

## What I'd do differently next time

- Add one clearly smoky/peated option for contrast.
- Keep the finale as something richer (sherry/port finish or older age statement).

---

## If you're buying blind

Start with easy, approachable bottles (sherried or honeyed styles work well) and keep one smoky option for contrast.
