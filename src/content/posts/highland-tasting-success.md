# Highland Tasting Success

Last Thursday evening, 24 whisky enthusiasts gathered at The George Hotel in Dorchester for what proved to be one of our most successful events of 2024. Our Highland Single Malt Experience, featuring premium expressions from Glenmorangie distillery, not only educated palates but raised an impressive £800 for Dorset Wildlife Trust.

## An Evening of Discovery

The evening began with a warm welcome from our resident expert, Robert Davies, who guided attendees through the rich history of Highland whisky making. We were particularly fortunate to have Sarah Campbell, Brand Ambassador from Glenmorangie, join us via video link to share insights into their unique production methods.

### Whiskies Tasted

- **Glenmorangie Original 10 Year Old**: The perfect introduction showcased the distillery's signature style
- **Glenmorangie Lasanta**: Sherry cask finishing added layers of complexity
- **Glenmorangie Quinta Ruban**: Port cask influence brought beautiful fruit notes
- **Glenmorangie 18 Year Old**: The evening's highlight demonstrated maturation mastery

## Supporting Conservation

All proceeds from the evening will directly support Dorset Wildlife Trust's conservation efforts across our beautiful county. Their work protecting habitats for endangered species like the great crested newt and Dorset heath fritillary butterfly is vital for future generations.
