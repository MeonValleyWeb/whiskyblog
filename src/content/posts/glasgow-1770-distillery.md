# The Glasgow Distillery (tasting notes)

Glasgow's whisky story is a modern one — and that's part of the appeal. These notes are from a **virtual tasting** focused on the Glasgow 1770 range and a couple of variations.

---

## What stood out

- The core style feels **clean and contemporary**: bright malt, citrus, and steady oak.
- The peated variant uses smoke like seasoning: **present, but not aggressive**.
- Higher-proof pours were where the texture and flavour layering really showed.

---

## If you're buying blind

Start with the core **Glasgow 1770 Single Malt** to learn the house style, then jump to the peated variant if you want something more characterful.
