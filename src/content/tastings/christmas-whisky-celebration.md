# Christmas Whisky Celebration

Ring in the festive season with our annual Christmas whisky celebration. This special evening combines exceptional whiskies with seasonal flavors, creating the perfect atmosphere for holiday cheer and whisky appreciation.

## A Festive Evening

Our Christmas celebration is more than just a tasting – it's a gathering of the Wessex Whisky Club community to celebrate another year of exceptional whiskies and charitable giving. Expect festive decorations, seasonal music, and the warm hospitality that makes our events special.

## Seasonal Whisky Selection

Tonight's whiskies have been carefully chosen to complement the festive season:

1. **Spiced Highland Single Malt** - Warming notes of cinnamon and nutmeg
2. **Sherry Cask Christmas Dram** - Rich fruit cake flavors perfect for the season
3. **Peated Winter Warmer** - Smoky comfort for cold December nights
4. **Honey & Vanilla Blend** - Sweet and smooth like Christmas morning
5. **Vintage Port Cask Finish** - A luxurious finale to the evening

## Festive Food Pairings

Each whisky will be paired with seasonal treats:
- Traditional mince pies and Christmas cake
- Artisan cheeses with cranberry chutney
- Smoked salmon canapés
- Chocolate truffles and shortbread
- Mulled wine welcome drink

## Evening Program

- **6:00 PM**: Mulled wine reception and festive canapés
- **6:30 PM**: Welcome and year in review
- **7:00 PM**: Guided tasting with seasonal pairings
- **8:00 PM**: Christmas raffle and prizes
- **8:30 PM**: Informal networking and celebration
- **9:00 PM**: Event conclusion

## Supporting Hants and IOW Air Ambulance

Our Christmas celebration continues our tradition of supporting Hants and IOW Air Ambulance, helping to provide life-saving services throughout the festive season and beyond.

## Special Features

- **Christmas Raffle**: Win whisky prizes and experiences
- **Year in Review**: Celebrating our 2024 achievements
- **Member Recognition**: Thanking our loyal supporters
- **Festive Atmosphere**: Full Christmas decorations and seasonal music

## Venue

The Pig Hotel in Brockenhurst provides the perfect countryside setting for our Christmas celebration, with its cozy atmosphere and excellent facilities in the heart of the New Forest.
