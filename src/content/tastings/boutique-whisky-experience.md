# Boutique Whisky Experience

Step into the world of independent bottling with this exclusive tasting featuring rare single cask expressions from some of Scotland's most respected independent bottlers. Each whisky tells a unique story of craftsmanship and character.

## What Makes This Special

Independent bottlers select exceptional casks from distilleries across Scotland, often offering unique expressions that showcase the distillery's character in new and exciting ways. These limited releases provide whisky enthusiasts with access to rare and distinctive drams.

## Featured Bottlers

Tonight's selection includes expressions from renowned independent bottlers such as:
- **Signatory Vintage** - Scotland's largest independent bottler
- **Gordon & MacPhail** - Over 125 years of whisky expertise
- **Cadenhead's** - Scotland's oldest independent bottler
- **The Whisky Agency** - Boutique selections from exceptional casks

## Tasting Selection

1. **Single Cask Speyside** - A 15-year-old gem from an undisclosed distillery
2. **Cask Strength Islay** - Unfiltered power from the whisky island
3. **Rare Highland Expression** - A 20-year-old single cask beauty
4. **Sherried Lowland** - Elegant complexity from first-fill sherry casks
5. **Mystery Dram** - A blind tasting challenge to test your palate

## Evening Format

- **7:30 PM**: Welcome reception with canapés
- **8:00 PM**: Introduction to independent bottling
- **8:30 PM**: Guided tasting of five exceptional whiskies
- **9:30 PM**: Discussion and Q&A
- **10:00 PM**: Event conclusion

## Supporting Hants and IOW Air Ambulance

This intimate evening supports the vital work of Hants and IOW Air Ambulance, bringing life-saving medical care to those who need it most across Hampshire and the Isle of Wight.

## Venue Information

The Crown Hotel in Blandford Forum provides an elegant and intimate setting for this exclusive tasting, with its historic charm and excellent hospitality.
