# Highland Single Malt Experience

An exceptional evening dedicated to the art of Highland single malt whisky. Our expert guide from Glenmorangie will take you on a journey through the Scottish Highlands, exploring the unique characteristics that make these whiskies so special.

## What to Expect

- **Welcome Reception**: Arrival drink and networking with fellow enthusiasts
- **Guided Tasting**: Four premium Highland single malts from Glenmorangie
- **Expert Insights**: Learn about distillation, maturation, and the Highland region
- **Food Pairing**: Specially selected Scottish cheeses and oatcakes
- **Q&A Session**: Direct access to distillery representatives

## Whiskies Featured

1. **Glenmorangie Original (10 Year Old)** - The perfect introduction to Highland single malt
2. **Glenmorangie Lasanta (12 Year Old)** - Sherry cask finished elegance
3. **Glenmorangie Quinta Ruban (12 Year Old)** - Port cask complexity
4. **Glenmorangie 18 Year Old** - The pinnacle of Highland craftsmanship

## Supporting Dorset Wildlife Trust

All proceeds from this event will support the vital conservation work of Dorset Wildlife Trust, helping to protect our local wildlife and natural habitats for future generations.
