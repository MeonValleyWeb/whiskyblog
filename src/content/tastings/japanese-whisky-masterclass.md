# Japanese Whisky Masterclass

Embark on a journey to the Land of the Rising Sun without leaving Devon. This exclusive masterclass explores the meticulous craftsmanship and unique philosophy behind Japanese whisky making.

## About Japanese Whisky

Japanese whisky combines Scottish traditions with Japanese precision and attention to detail. The result is a category of spirits that has taken the world by storm, winning international awards and capturing the hearts of whisky enthusiasts globally.

## Featured Distilleries

### Nikka Whisky
Founded by Masataka Taketsuru, who studied whisky making in Scotland, Nikka represents the perfect marriage of Scottish knowledge and Japanese craftsmanship.

### Suntory
Japan's oldest whisky company, Suntory has been perfecting the art of Japanese whisky since 1923, creating some of the world's most sought-after expressions.

## Tasting Selection

1. **Nikka From The Barrel** - A rich, complex blend showcasing Nikka's expertise
2. **Yamazaki 12 Year Old** - Suntory's flagship single malt (subject to availability)
3. **Hakushu 12 Year Old** - The fresh, green notes of Japan's forest distillery
4. **Hibiki Japanese Harmony** - The pinnacle of Japanese blending art
5. **Special Reserve Expression** - A rare bottling exclusive to this event
