# 🏝️ Kilchoman Islay Single Malt Experience

Journey to the **windswept shores of Islay** without leaving Devon. This exclusive tasting explores the distinctive character of Kilchoman, Islay's farm distillery, known for their traditional methods and exceptional single malts.

---

## 🚜 About Kilchoman Distillery

**Kilchoman is Islay's farm distillery**, established in 2005 as the first new distillery on Islay for 124 years. They grow their own barley, malt it on-site, and mature their whisky in traditional dunnage warehouses, creating truly authentic **farm-to-bottle single malts**.

**What makes them unique:**
- First new Islay distillery in 124 years
- Complete farm-to-bottle production
- Own barley grown on-site
- Traditional floor malting
- Dunnage warehouse maturation

---

## 📖 The Kilchoman Story

Founded by **Anthony Wills**, Kilchoman represents a return to traditional whisky making methods. From growing barley on their own farm to bottling the final product, they control every aspect of production, resulting in whiskies with **genuine provenance and character**.

**The Philosophy:**
- Traditional methods meet modern quality
- Complete control over production
- Authentic Islay character
- Sustainable farming practices
- Small batch excellence

## 🌊 Understanding Islay Character

**What to Expect from Islay Whiskies:**

**Peat Influence:** Varying levels of smokiness from the island's peat bogs  
**Maritime Character:** Sea salt and coastal influences  
**Complexity:** Layers of flavor from traditional production methods  
**Balance:** Despite intensity, well-balanced and approachable  

**Tasting Tips:**
- Add a few drops of water to open up the flavors
- Take time between drams to appreciate each whisky
- Notice how the maritime location influences the taste
